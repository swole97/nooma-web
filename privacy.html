Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Nooma, we believe your health data is yours alone. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

The short version

  • Your medication data stays on your device
  • We never sell your data to anyone
  • No ads, no third-party trackers
  • We collect minimal anonymous analytics to improve the app

What we collect

Data stored on your device only:

  • Medication names, dosages, and schedules
  • Reminder times and history
  • Your companion's name and customizations

This data never leaves your phone. We cannot see it, and we don't want to.

Anonymous analytics we may collect:

  • App opens and feature usage (e.g., "40% of users use snooze")
  • Crash reports to fix bugs
  • Device type and OS version

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It's never tied to you or your medications.

What we don't collect

  • Your name, email, or contact information (unless you contact us)
  • Health records or medical history
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal data

Third parties

We do not share, sell, or provide your data to any third parties. No advertisers, no data brokers, no insurance companies. Ever.

Notifications

Nooma sends local notifications to remind you about your medications. These are processed entirely on your device. The notification content never includes medication names on your lock screen — just a gentle note from your companion.

Data security

All data stored on your device uses your phone's built-in encryption. If you use a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID, your Nooma data is protected by that same security.

Children's privacy

Nooma is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes, we'll notify you in the app before they take effect. Minor clarifications may be made without notice.

Contact us

Questions about privacy? Email us at hello@nooma.care

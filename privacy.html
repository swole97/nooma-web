Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Nooma is a medication-reminder app operated by Simonas Kralikas, an individual developer based in Lithuania (EU). This policy explains what we collect, why, how it's stored, and the rights you have. Questions: hello@nooma.care.

The short version

  • You create an account, and your data (including the medications you add) is stored securely in the cloud so it syncs and isn't lost if you change phones.
  • We use a small number of trusted service providers to run the app (see below).
  • We never sell your data.
  • The app shows ads to users on the free tier.
  • You can delete your account and data from inside the app at any time.

What we collect

Account information

  • Your email address, and (if you sign in with Google or Apple) the basic profile info those services return. Used to create and secure your account.

The information you enter in the app

  • Medication names, dosages and schedules, reminder times and history, and your companion's name and customizations.
  • Because medication information relates to your health, we treat it as sensitive ("special category") data and process it only with your consent, which you provide by entering it to use the app's reminder features.

Purchases

  • If you subscribe or buy in-app items, our payments provider processes the transaction. We receive your subscription/purchase status, not your card details.

Diagnostics & device data

  • Crash reports and basic technical data (device type, OS version) to fix bugs.
  • An advertising identifier, used to show ads on the free tier (see "Ads").

Where your data is stored

Your account and the information you enter are stored in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore), not solely on your device. This lets your data sync across devices and survive reinstalls. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Service providers we share data with

We don't sell your data. We share the minimum necessary with providers that help us run the app:

  • Google Firebase (Google) — accounts, cloud database, and push notifications.
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In — optional sign-in methods.
  • RevenueCat — manages subscriptions and purchases.
  • Google AdMob (Google) — shows ads to free-tier users.
  • Sentry — crash and error reporting.
  • Expo — app notifications and delivery.

Some of these providers are based outside the EU (e.g. the United States); where that happens, transfers are covered by appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

Ads

Free-tier users see ads provided by Google AdMob, which may use an advertising identifier to show and measure ads. Where required, we ask for your consent first, and you can reset or limit ad tracking in your device settings. Premium subscribers don't see ads.

Notifications

Reminders are designed for privacy: notifications never include the medication name — just a gentle nudge from your companion.

How long we keep your data

We keep your data until you delete your account. When you delete your account, your account and the information you entered are removed. Crash logs are retained for a short period (around 90 days) and backups are purged shortly after deletion.

Deleting your account

You can delete your account and associated data at any time from Settings → Delete account in the app.

Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your data, to data portability, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these, email hello@nooma.care. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority — in Lithuania, the State Data Protection Inspectorate (Valstybinė duomenų apsaugos inspekcija).

Security

Data is protected with encryption in transit and at rest, and access is restricted. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Children

Nooma is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we'll remove it.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes, we'll notify you in the app before they take effect.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at hello@nooma.care

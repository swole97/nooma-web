the common ones

"my notifications aren't coming through"

nine times out of ten, this is a phone setting, not the app. quick checklist:

  • on iPhone: Settings → Notifications → Nooma → make sure "Allow Notifications" is on, "Sounds" and "Banners" are enabled
  • on Android: Settings → Apps → Nooma → Notifications → make sure the channel is on
  • if you have Focus Mode / Do Not Disturb on, add Nooma to your allowed apps
  • battery saver modes (especially on Android) can silently block scheduled reminders — add Nooma to your exceptions

if you've checked all of this and they're still not coming through, email us with your phone model and OS version and we'll dig in.

"i added a medication but it's not showing up"

medications added to a time slot show up on the home screen at the time you set. if you added one for "evening" and it's currently morning, you'll see it later. tap the "meds" tab to see all of your medications.

"i missed a dose. did i break anything?"

no. nooma doesn't have streaks, chains, or punishments. missing a dose doesn't break a thing. you can tap the dose card in history and mark it as taken late if you want — that's it.

"i want to cancel my Plus subscription"

cancellations are handled by Apple / Google, not by us:

  • on iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Nooma Plus → Cancel
  • on Android: Play Store → Profile → Payments and subscriptions → Subscriptions → Nooma Plus → Cancel

your Plus features stay active until the end of the billing period.

"i want a refund"

refunds are handled by Apple / Google directly. Apple has a refund request page at reportaproblem.apple.com. for Google, go to your order history in Play Store.

if there's something specific we can help with — a feature you expected that wasn't there, a bug that ruined your experience — please email us first. we're real people and we read everything.

"is my data private?"

your medication info is stored encrypted. notifications never show medication names on your lock screen. we don't sell your data, ever.

for the full version, see our privacy policy.

"i want to delete my account"

we don't currently have an in-app delete button — we know, we're working on it. for now, email us at hello@nooma.care with "delete my account" in the subject and we'll wipe everything within 7 days.

you can also visit our account deletion page for more details.

"i changed phones / lost my data"

right now nooma keeps your data on your phone — which means it's private, but it doesn't transfer between devices. if you've changed phones, you'll need to re-add your medications. we're building proper backup soon.

"is nooma actually free?"

yes. the medical part of nooma — reminders, medications, dose tracking, history, refill reminders — is free forever. no paywalls, no limits.

nooma Plus is an optional subscription (£5.99/month or £49/year) that adds cosmetics, scene packs, and faster mission rewards. you never need to pay to use nooma for medication reminders.

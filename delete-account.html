Delete Your Account

Request deletion of your Nooma account and associated data

To request deletion of your Nooma account and all associated data, please email us:

hello@nooma.care

Please include the email address associated with your account in your request.

What will be deleted

  • Your account information
  • App usage data
  • Saved preferences and settings
  • Your companion's name and customizations

What may be retained

We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Purchase and subscription records (for legal and accounting purposes)
  • Data required to comply with legal obligations

Processing time

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process, please contact us at hello@nooma.care
